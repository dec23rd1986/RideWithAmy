About

Amy is a regular gal...and spinning fool! Amy wants to make you smile--AND SWEAT! A newcomer to organized fitness of any kind, Amy joined Revolution San Antonio in February 2016 and it quickly became her happy place. The welcoming staff, friendly riders, motivating instructors, and great music became a bright spot in her day and after each class she felt strong, confident, stress-free, and happy! Amy is proud to be a part of REV as an instructor and hopes to brighten your day and challenge your workouts by adding her own brand of fun-loving "dork swagger." Her light-hearted attitude and eclectic playlists draw from all genres and decades of music in a random, "iPod on shuffle" kind of style.
